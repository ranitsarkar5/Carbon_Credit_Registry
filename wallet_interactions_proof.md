## 🔒 Verification & Authentication Standards
Every interaction listed above represents a cryptographically signed transaction verified by the Soroban Virtual Machine (SVM). 
The transactions enforce role-based permissions:
1. **Auditor checks:** Telemetry inputs are only registered if the calling public key matches a whitelisted Auditor in the contract persistent storage.
2. **Signature checks:** Project Owners must approve and sign `mint_credits` with their private keys before CCT tokens are minted to their wallets.
